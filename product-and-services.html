  • Fire Alarm System – We are considered as one of the leading of fire alarm system that are made using quality material in keeping in mind the requirements of our clientsA Fire alarm system has a number of devices working together to detect and warn people through visual and audio appliances when smoke, fire, or other emergencies are present. We can provide all types of Fire Alarm System including Siren System, PA Talk back system, Addressable System , Non Addressable System, Smoke detection System , Heat detection.
  • Riser System – Vertical Riser system is hydrant system installed in building having fire safety solution on each floor of building . We can provide both Dry Riser System and Wet Riser System.
  • Sprinkler System – A Fire Sprinkler System is an active fire protection method, consisting of a water supply system, providing adequate pressure and flowrate to a water distribution piping system, onto which fire sprinklers are connected.

These fire systems can easily detect fire in the initial stage and can be controlled automatically by dialing to the fire brigade authority. These projects are handled by our qualified team of personnel, who have vast experience in their respective field. Highly cost effective, our range of systems is known for their reliability and durable performance.
  • Pumping System – We are instrumental in offering our clients a pumping system .A fire pump is a part of a fire hydrant system and sprinkler system’s water supply and Powered by electric and Diesel. The pump intake is either connected to the underground water.
  • Fire Extinguisher – A fire extinguisher is an active fire protection device used to extinguish or control small fires, often in emergency situations .
  • Fire Doors – A fire door is a door with a fire-resistance rating used as part of a passive fire protection system to reduce the spread of fire and smoke between separate compartments of a building.
